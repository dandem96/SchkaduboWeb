SckaduboWeb

Once complete, you will be able to use SchkaduboWeb to help a fictitious Nigerian king reclaim his kingdom. Learn more about the great works of the Schkadubo Dynasty, the story of his people, and the epic challenges faced by the Nigerian Empire and Uyo-Uson. See if you can be of help to the King and prove yourself and your tribe to His Excellency. Expect no reward but to be remembered forever as a hero to the Schkadubo family and the people of Uyo-Uson.

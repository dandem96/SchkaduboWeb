Biography:


Sannu da zuwa! My name is Dodo Schkadubo. I was born in Kemyllawana in 1976 and was declared Crown Prince that December in a great ceremony. There, I was given tribute from all the peoples of Oyo-Uson, including the Ma-uwa and Hanisdan tribes who warred for centuries before by grandfather Haushan I was crowned King by the Nigerian Emperor.

I was crowned King Schkadubo I in 2010 in an equally great ceremony; was crownded by the Emperor himself, whom I pledged allegiance to. Within three years I made numerous reforms and great works. These include the following:


  • The Great Stadium of Uyo
  • Universal Good Housing Act of 2011
  • The Schkadubo National Library
